About Me

I am an aspiring GIS professional who enjoys data visualisation/analytics, coding, and cartography. I enjoy the broad spectrum of disciplines the geospatial industry offers. I have strong technical and written skills and an academic background in Geospatial Science and Surveying. My undergraduate thesis is assessing seasonal variations of seagrass extents in the Port Phillip Bay using satellite imagery. I am graduating in October 2022 and looking forward to entering the geospatial industry in a full-time capacity.

Proficient: ArcMap, ArcGIS, QGIS, Excel
Knowledgeable: Java Script, html, Linux/Windows/WSL, Django, Python, Databases (PostgreSQL/PgAdmin), SQL, AutoCAD
Emerging: FME, Adobe Creative Suite, Docker

In my spare time I play ice-hockey for Australia. As well as playing for the Melbourne Ice Women's team in the Australian Women's Ice Hockey League. I am a level 1 accredited coach, coaching all levels of hockey from grassroots to premier divisions.
I am a current member of the Ice Hockey Victoria Women's Committee. Working towards the goal of increasing female participation and driving towards gender equity in the sport through the development and execution of grassroots, recruitment, and player pathways for women.
