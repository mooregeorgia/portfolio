About Me

I am an aspiring GIS professional who enjoys data visualisation/analytics, coding, and cartography. I enjoy the broad spectrum of disciplines the geospatial industry offers. I have strong technical and written skills and an academic background in Geospatial Science and Surveying. My undergraduate thesis is assessing seasonal variation of seagrass extent in the Port Phillip Bay (2017 to 2022) using Sentinel satellite imagery. I am graduating in October 2022 and looking forward to entering the geospatial industry in a full-time capacity.

Proficient: ArcMap, ArcGIS, QGIS, Excel
Knowledgeable: Java Script, html, Linux/Windows/WSL, Django, Python, Databases (PostgreSQL/PgAdmin), SQL, AutoCAD
